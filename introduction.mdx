## Welcome to Production Inspection Form

Production Inspection Form is a Next.js-based web application designed for technical inspection of production equipment. Built with React and Next.js, it provides a streamlined interface for conducting equipment inspections, tracking maintenance activities, and ensuring quality control in manufacturing environments.

## Use Cases

The Production Inspection Form system is designed for manufacturing and production environments where regular equipment inspections are critical for:

- **Quality Control**: Ensure equipment meets operational standards through systematic inspections
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Track equipment condition and identify issues before they become critical
- **Compliance**: Maintain inspection records for regulatory compliance and auditing
- **Production Monitoring**: Monitor equipment status across divisions, areas, and zones
- **Technical Documentation**: Create detailed inspection reports with observations and notes

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Login">
    Technicians log in with their credentials to access the inspection system
  </Step>
  <Step title="Equipment Selection">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspection Checklist">
    Complete the dynamic inspection checklist specific to the equipment category
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Results">
    Add observations and submit the inspection form to the backend system
  </Step>
